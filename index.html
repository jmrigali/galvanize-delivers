Locally Sourced

We grab local animals from the side of the road and grill them up. These animals may have left us suddenly, but we can celebrate their lives by preparing their bodies in the most delicious of fashions

Economically Friendly

By removing the deceased animals from the side of the road, we are cleaning up the city from the sites of death. We're sheltering children from these horrific tragedies

visibility

Accessible

If you have a recently deceased animal in your neighborhood, give us a call! We will pick it up and give it a proper end of life ceremony and... bonus for you! We will provide a discounted meal for you and your family!
